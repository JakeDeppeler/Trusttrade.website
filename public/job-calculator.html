Wages

Only you can see this page — enforced in the database, not just hidden
NameRole In van Pay $/hr On-cost %super, etc.Billable %on the tools School~1 wk/moSubsidy $ True $/hr
Billable % is the honest one. 100 = full-time on the tools. 60 = they're quoting half the week. The non-billable share of every wage flows into overhead automatically — which is exactly where it belongs, and exactly what most trades forget.
On-cost % is everything you pay on top of the base hourly wage — super, WorkCover, leave loading, payroll tax. Around 25% is a solid starting point. It already includes super, so don't also add super as a separate overhead — you'd be counting it twice.
In van = who's actually out on the tools. Only people in a van earn billable hours, and your active crews (on the Targets page) come straight from this count. Office / Admin can't be charged out — their whole wage flows into overhead. School is its own bracket: pick Apprentice and it ticks on automatically (~one week a month); change them off Apprentice and it clears. Got a tradesperson who's studying? Just tick School to keep the 60 days.
